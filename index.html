More About Us

Our Awesome Services.

Utilizing the CONZULT's collaborative approach, CONZULT provides the leaders of organizations with professional services that assist in the development of their organization. Conzults’ work covers a variety of sectors including health, education, agriculture, poverty reduction, WASH, markets and value chains, microfinance, and livelihoods. Our areas of work within these sectors include:

Strategy Development.

Consulting and assessing options for the design and development of robust contextualized programs including strategic planning, business plans, curriculum development, policy advocacy.

Capacity Development.

Teaching and supporting capacity development processes in order to ensure that programs are successfully implemented and continue through an ongoing process of staff training, team building and leadership development.

Evaluation and Social Research.

Reviewing and evaluating programs, assessing the impact of a program, and extracting results. Results can take the form of program analysis, baseline studies, monitoring /evaluation, impact assessment and social research studies.

Logistics support.

Providing on location support for international organizations and individuals interested in working in Rwanda. Includes arranging accommodation, transportation, introducing and scheduling meetings with key stakeholders, interpretation, headhunting, fieldwork, additional services as required.

Who Are We.

Conzult WAS CREATED TO PROVIDE A SOUNDING BOARD TO LEADERS OF LOCAL NGOS. THROUGH STRATEGY SESSIONS, GROUP DIALOGUE, AND CONSULTING SERVICES, Conzult ASSISTS ORGANIZATIONS IN ACHIEVING THEIR RESPECTIVE GOALS AND VISION.

Our Mission.

Our mission is to partner with local organizations to assist in achieving their fullest organizational potential.

Our Values.

Conzult values strong relationships developed over time through long-term partnerships, input of beneficiaries and utilizing local knowledge and resources to create long lasting sustainable solutions, and Through a responsive bottom-up approach Conzult values grassroots organizations and their commitment to affecting change at all societal levels.

Our Strategy.

Having the ability to create and nurture connections at the grassroots level and networking with larger organizations is the source of our success. We act as the connection between larger organizations and foundations and the Community Based Organizations (CBOs) that work directly with the community. We strive to ensure that the connection between larger organizations and CBOs will be a mutual exchange where all partners involved gain equally.

Our Vision.

.NGOs improving the quality of life of beneficiaries. .More effective and efficient organizations that will achieve a higher impact. .Local organizations better prepared to advocate for themselves in the development community. .Experts coming together to meet specific needs of organizations in a holistic manner.
